A great club. A digital presence that wasn't keeping up.

  • ClientGlentoran FC
  • SectorSport & Community
  • ServicesWeb, Digital Marketing, Systems
  • Duration4 months

+41% ticket sales.
3× GlensTV growth. Built for mobile from the ground up.

Read the full case study

Glentoran is one of Ireland's most decorated football clubs — 23 league titles, a rebuilt stadium, a social partnership doing genuine community work. But their digital presence had grown without direction, and it showed.

Fans looking for tickets ended up in the same navigation as commercial sponsors. The mobile experience — where most of their audience lives — was an afterthought. GlensTV, their own streaming platform, was barely discoverable.

We rebuilt the site around the fan first, restructured the content architecture for every audience segment, repositioned GlensTV as a core benefit, and integrated ticketing, loyalty, and supporter data into a single coherent system.
