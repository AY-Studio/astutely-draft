Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Astutely ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of everyone who visits our website or enquires about our services. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and what we do with it.

We are the data controller for personal data collected through this website. If you have any questions, you can contact us at hello@astutely.co.uk.

1. What data we collect

Contact and enquiry data

When you submit an enquiry through our contact form or email us directly, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your company name (if provided)
  • The content of your message

Newsletter and communications

If you subscribe to our newsletter or insights updates, we collect your email address and, optionally, your name.

Website usage data

We use analytics tools to understand how visitors use our website. This may include pages visited, time on site, referring source, and general geographic location (country/region level). This data is aggregated and not used to identify individuals.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies. Please see Section 6 below for full details.

2. How we use your data

  • To respond to enquiries — when you contact us, we use your details to reply and discuss your project
  • To send insights and updates — if you've opted in, we'll send occasional emails about our thinking and work
  • To improve our website — anonymised analytics help us understand what's working and what isn't
  • To fulfil client work — if you become a client, we process your data as necessary to deliver the agreed services

3. Legal basis for processing

Under UK GDPR, we process your personal data on the following legal grounds:

  • Legitimate interests — for responding to enquiries and for analytics that help us improve our services
  • Consent — for newsletter sign-ups and non-essential cookies
  • Contract performance — for data processed as part of delivering client services

4. Who we share your data with

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with trusted third-party services that help us operate, including:

  • Email service providers — to manage newsletter subscriptions and send communications
  • Hosting and infrastructure — our website is hosted on Netlify, which processes data in accordance with their own privacy policy
  • Analytics providers — we may use tools such as Google Analytics; data is anonymised where possible

All third-party providers are required to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we've specified.

5. How long we keep your data

  • Enquiry data — we retain correspondence for up to 3 years, or longer if it relates to an ongoing client relationship
  • Newsletter subscriptions — until you unsubscribe
  • Analytics data — typically retained for 26 months in line with standard analytics provider settings

6. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. We use the following types:

  • Strictly necessary cookies — required for the site to function. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use the site. These are only set with your consent.
  • Preference cookies — remember your cookie consent choice.

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time using the banner shown on your first visit, or by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectify any inaccurate data
  • Erase your data (the "right to be forgotten"), where applicable
  • Restrict how we process your data
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@astutely.co.uk. We will respond within one calendar month.

8. Complaints

If you have concerns about how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk or by calling 0303 123 1113.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last revised. Significant changes will be communicated where appropriate.

10. Contact us

For any privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: hello@astutely.co.uk
  • Website: astutely.co.uk/contact
